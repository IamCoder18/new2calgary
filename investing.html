📚 Understanding Canadian Accounts

TFSA (Tax-Free Savings Account)

Best for most people. Growth is tax-free!

  • 2026 contribution limit: ~$7,000
  • Can withdraw anytime, tax-free
  • Great for short and long-term goals
  • Recommended: Start here!

RRSP (Registered Retirement Savings Plan)

For retirement savings. Contributions reduce your taxes.

  • 2026 contribution limit: ~$32,000 (or 18% of income)
  • Tax-deferred growth
  • Best for long-term retirement
  • Withdrawals are taxed

FHSA (First Home Savings Account)

NEW! For first-time home buyers. Best of both worlds!

  • Up to $40,000 lifetime contribution
  • Tax-deductible contributions
  • Tax-free withdrawals for home purchase
  • Must be first-time buyer

💡 Getting Started Tips

  • Start small - Even $50/month adds up over time
  • Use index funds/ETFs - Diversify with low fees
  • Automate - Set up automatic contributions
  • Don't panic - Market drops are normal, stay the course
  • Use referrals - Get free money to start!
  • Don't invest money you need soon - Keep emergency fund separate
